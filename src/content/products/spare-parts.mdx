SharkJaw genuine spare parts keep your shark jaw and towing pin systems operating at rated capacity throughout the vessel's service life. Using factory-sourced parts ensures dimensional and material compliance with the original class-approved design — which matters for safety-critical deck equipment operating at high load in offshore conditions.

**Jaw Insert Plates** are the primary wear items in a shark jaw system. Manufactured from quenched and tempered alloy steel with a hardness optimised for abrasion resistance against anchor chain, they are available in the full range of chain diameter profiles across all SJ models. Most vessels replace insert plates every 12–24 months depending on chain type and operating cycle frequency.

**Seals & O-Rings** — complete seal kits for hydraulic cylinders, rotary actuators, and manifold block assemblies are available by equipment model. Kits are pre-packaged with all seals required for a full cylinder overhaul and are labelled by position to simplify assembly.

**Pins & Bushings** — load-bearing pivot pins and replaceable bronze bushings for shark jaw hinge points and towing pin base assemblies. These components are inspected during annual surveys and typically replaced every 3–5 years depending on load history.

**Hydraulic Valves & Hoses** — solenoid valves, pressure relief valves, and quick-release valve assemblies are supplied as complete sub-assemblies for straightforward exchange. Hydraulic hoses are available in standard lengths and can be custom-fabricated to vessel-specific routing dimensions.

**Control Panel Components** — PLC modules, solenoid coils, limit switches, and indicator lamps for the deck control and wheelhouse remote panels. Stocked for all current production models and available for most equipment manufactured from 2018 onwards.

All parts are dispatched with a material traceability document. Class-witnessed inspection and certification is available on request for insert plates and structural pins destined for vessels under survey.
