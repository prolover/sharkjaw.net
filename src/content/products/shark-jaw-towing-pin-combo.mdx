The SharkJaw combo system brings the shark jaw and towing pin together into a single integrated deck equipment package, engineered and supplied by one manufacturer and covered under a single class approval certificate. This approach is particularly well suited to AHTS new builds and to retrofit projects where both pieces of equipment are being replaced at the same time.

At the heart of the combo system is a shared hydraulic power unit matched to the combined requirements of both the shark jaw and the towing pin. Rather than maintaining two separate HPUs with separate hydraulic circuits, spare parts inventories, and service records, the vessel operator has a single power source with a consolidated manifold, dual-circuit priority valving, and one set of filters and accumulators to maintain. The reduction in installed hydraulic hardware lowers both the capital cost and the long-term maintenance burden.

Integrated combo systems are available in two configuration families. The **Triplex + Retractable** configuration pairs the heavy-duty triplex shark jaw with a hydraulic retractable towing pin — the preferred choice for larger AHTS and ocean towing vessels where maximum holding capacity and a clear working deck are both required. The **Fork + Fixed** configuration combines the compact fork-type shark jaw with a fixed towing pin and is optimised for harbour tugs and coastal workboats where a smaller deck footprint and lower cost take priority. Both configurations use the same PLC control platform, providing consistent operator interface across a fleet.
